Substellar InvestiGATORS

Group Research

The Substellar InvestiGATORS Group at SFSU uses observational and theoretical based approaches via spectral energy distributions and atmospheric retrievals to understand the complex atmospheres of substellar sources. Some current projects topics inlcude

  • Characterizing Atmospheres at the Intersection of Directly-Imaged Exoplanets and Brown Dwarfs
  • Creating a metallicity sequence for substellar objects
  • Characterizing Wide L+T binaries
  • Characterizing benchmark brown dwarfs
