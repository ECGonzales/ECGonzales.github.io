I'm a physics PhD candiate based at the City University of New York Graduate Center in New York City, working with the Brown Dwarfs in New York City (BDNYC) research group. My research focuses on understanding the atmospheres of brown dwarfs and low-mass stars. I am a former LSSTC Data Science Fellow and a former Research Mentor in the Science Research Mentoring Program (SRMP) at the American Museum of Natural History.
