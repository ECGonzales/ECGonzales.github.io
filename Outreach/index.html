Outreach

Black In Physics

I have been focusing on orgainzing the inagural #BlackInPhysics week October 25-31, 2020, a week dedicated to celebreating and connecting Black physicists. During this week we aim to connect Black physicists and the larger community through professional and social events, daily essays, and community engagement on Twitter. To learn more about Black In Physics and #BlackInPhysics week checkout blackinphysics.org and @BlackInPhysics on Twitter.
